25% off Canadian Resident Admission
September 4th, 2024 Anne Dougherty Canadian Deals & Coupons

Walt Disney World has a promotion for Canadian residents that will begin on September the 10th. Canadian residents can save 25% or more on a 4-day or longer Walt Disney World theme park ticket. Beginning September the 10th you can purchase tickets for select dates in late 2024 and 2025. You will need to select the start date for your tickets at the time of purchase. 2024 dates range from November the 10th through December the 14th, and 2025 dates are between January the 5th and September the 27th.

Tickets can be purchased through travel agents, or by calling (407) W-DISNEY or (407) 934-7639 to speak with a vacation planning expert. Tickets are valid for admission beginning on the selected start date, but they do not have to be used on consecutive days. Multi-day tickets are valid 2-4 days longer than the number of days of admission.
