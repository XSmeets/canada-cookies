Wedding Season

Celebrate wedding season in style with John Lewis. Make a statement with the perfect ensemble from our collection of women's wedding fashion. We offer stunning wedding dresses as well as bridesmaid dresses, whilst for those attending your big day, our curated selection of wedding guest dresses features an array of timeless designs and contemporary cuts. Add a flourish from our selection of wedding hats and fascinators, and complete your ensemble with our stunning range of wedding guest shoes. From classic pumps to chic sandals, we have everything you need to dance the night away. You can even browse our wedding outfit rental options for a selection of stunning attire. For little ones, you can pick from adorable flower girl outfits and page boy ensembles that are sure to melt hearts and complement the wedding party. Dress the groom and his party with our men's wedding fashion collection. From impeccably tailored men’s wedding suits to sleek accessories, ensure they look their best as they stand by the bride's side. If you’re looking for the perfect present, our range of wedding gifts includes useful homeware alongside personalised keepsakes, to help you find the ideal token to celebrate the happy couple's union.
